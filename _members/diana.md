Diana graduated from the University of Idaho with a B.S. in biology and medical sciences in 2021. There she worked in multiple disciplines including plant pathology, microbiology and fish physiology. After that she moved to Denver CO and worked at National Jewish Health where her research centered around novel biased agonists for use as therapeutics in asthma. Now she is excited to incorporate her immunology background into research in epilepsy and SUDEP. In her free time she likes to read, bake and watch reality tv.
